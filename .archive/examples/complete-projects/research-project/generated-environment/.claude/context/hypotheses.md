## Hypothesis Testing Protocol

### 1. Data Requirements
- Minimum 30 studies per hypothesis
- Geographic representation from at least 5 regions
- Temporal coverage spanning at least 20 years

### 2. Statistical Tests
- **H1**: Correlation analysis, meta-regression
- **H2**: Linear regression, dose-response analysis
- **H3**: Spatial analysis, landscape metrics
- **H0**: Equivalence testing, Bayesian analysis

### 3. Validation Steps
- Sensitivity analysis
- Leave-one-out validation
- Publication bias assessment
- Heterogeneity testing (I² statistic)

### 4. Decision Criteria
- **Accept**: p < 0.05 and effect size meets threshold
- **Reject**: p > 0.05 or effect size below threshold
- **Inconclusive**: Insufficient data or high heterogeneity (I² > 75%)
