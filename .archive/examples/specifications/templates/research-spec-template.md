## Tips for This Template

✅ **DO**:
- State your research question as a clear, specific question
- Include concrete hypotheses with testable predictions
- Cite at least 3-5 key papers in literature review
- Specify exact statistical tests you'll use
- Include timeline with dates
- Mention academic publication goals

❌ **DON'T**:
- Say just "I want to analyze data" without research context
- Skip the hypothesis section
- Be vague about methodology
- Forget to mention IRB/ethics if working with human subjects
- Leave out literature review
- Use only generic terms like "study" or "investigation"
