## Tips for This Template

✅ **DO**:
- Use first-person language ("I", "my", "I want to")
- Be specific about your personal motivation
- Include concrete milestones with dates
- Describe your starting point honestly
- Mention tracking methods and accountability
- Use domain-specific keywords (fitness, learning, home, etc.)
- Set realistic timeline based on your life constraints

❌ **DON'T**:
- Write in third person or abstract terms
- Say just "self-improvement project" without specifics
- Skip the personal motivation section
- Be vague about time commitment
- Forget to identify obstacles and mitigation
- Leave out measurement/tracking plans
- Set impossible deadlines that ignore your real life
